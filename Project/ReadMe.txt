    => Instructions and info 

    1.  Press A or D t0 move paddle left or right.
    2.  Press P to pause the game.
    3.  Press C to continue the game.
    4.  Press S to save the game.
    5.  Press Q to quit the game.
    6.  Every hit gives you 1 score.
    7.  If your hit destroys brick you'll get 5 score.
    8.  After destroying the brick combo will increase by 1.
    9.  Top row of bricks have 5 health and keeps on
        decreasing in every preceding row.
    10. You have total three lives .
